Welcome to the Center for Applied Research in Information Technology

Welcome to the website of CARIT, the Center for Applied Research in Information Technology. The CARIT is an interdisciplinary research center that uses scientifically rigorous information technology approaches to conduct high-quality survey research, program evaluation, performance assessment, and experimental research. Our mission is to cooperate with other disciplines to discover innovative methods to use computing to tackle today's most difficult challenges. For professors in need of support with research projects, grants, or contracts, the CARIT is a great resource. Organizations and agencies can also use the CARIT for a number of purposes, including technology reviews and business analyses.

We hope that you will peruse the website to see what CARIT projects have been conducted or are in the works. You can also find information about the services available through CARIT.
